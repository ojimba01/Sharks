Here Are 5 Fun Facts About The Great White

  1. Great whites can detect one drop of blood in 25 gallons of water.
  2. The great white’s scientific moniker means “ragged-toothed.”
  3. The great white is warm-blooded. Most sharks are cold-blooded.
  4. Great whites are the largest predatory fish in the world.
  5. Like all sharks, great whites have no true bones.
